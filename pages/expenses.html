Business Expenses

Record business spending with correct vendor, account, category, and amount.

Purpose

Record money spent by the business for operations, materials, services, subscriptions, or equipment rentals.

Simple Example

An $800 Mastercard expense for A1 Rental is recorded under Equipment Rental with a description and memo.

Simple Bookkeeping Decision Rule

Record an expense when the business already paid. Use a bill instead when the company received an invoice but will pay later.
